## 基本概念

兩個都可以當「動詞的名詞化」，但有些動詞只能接其中一個。

- **動名詞（Gerund）**：V-ing — She enjoys **reading**.
- **不定詞（Infinitive）**：to + V — She decided **to read**.

---

## 只能接動名詞的動詞

這些動詞後面**只能接 V-ing**，不能接 to V：

- enjoy / finish / avoid / consider / suggest / recommend
- admit / deny / miss / practice / risk / keep / postpone / delay

- ✓ He **avoided making** eye contact.
- ✗ He **avoided to make** eye contact.

- ✓ She **suggested starting** earlier.
- ✗ She **suggested to start** earlier.

記法：這類動詞大多描述「已發生的事」或「習慣性行為」，用 -ing 帶有「正在進行」的感覺。

---

## 只能接不定詞的動詞

這些動詞後面**只能接 to V**：

- want / need / decide / plan / hope / expect / agree / refuse / manage
- attempt / tend / fail / promise / afford / claim / pretend

- ✓ They **decided to postpone** the experiment.
- ✗ They **decided postponing** the experiment.

- ✓ The study **failed to account** for several variables.
- ✗ The study **failed accounting** for several variables.

記法：這類動詞大多描述「未來的意圖或計畫」，to V 帶有「指向未來」的方向感。

---

## 兩者都可以，但意思不同

這是最難的部分，同一個動詞接 -ing 和 to V 意思完全不一樣。

### remember

- **remember to V** — 記得去做（還沒做）
  - Please **remember to submit** the form by Friday.

- **remember V-ing** — 記得曾經做過（已做過）
  - I **remember reading** this paper before.

### forget

- **forget to V** — 忘記要去做（沒做）
  - She **forgot to bring** the samples.

- **forget V-ing** — 忘記曾經做過（已做過）
  - He **forgot meeting** her at the conference.

### stop

- **stop to V** — 停下來，去做另一件事
  - The researcher **stopped to check** the equipment.（停下來去檢查）

- **stop V-ing** — 停止做某件事
  - The researcher **stopped checking** the data.（停止做檢查這件事）

### try

- **try to V** — 試圖做（有點難度，努力嘗試）
  - Scientists **tried to replicate** the results.（努力嘗試）

- **try V-ing** — 試試看（嘗試一種方法）
  - **Try adjusting** the temperature and see if it changes.（試試這個方法）

---

## 介系詞後面一律接動名詞

介系詞（in / by / for / about / without / after / before...）後面**只能接 V-ing**，不能接 to V。

- She succeeded **in solving** the problem.
- **Without knowing** the cause, we cannot find a cure.
- The results were obtained **by analyzing** satellite data.
- **After conducting** three experiments, the team reached a conclusion.

常見錯誤：

- ✗ She succeeded **in to solve** the problem.
- ✓ She succeeded **in solving** the problem.

---

## 主詞位置

V-ing 和 to V 都可以當主詞，但風格不同：

- **Studying** abroad broadens your perspective.（動名詞當主詞，較口語）
- **To study** abroad is a valuable experience.（不定詞當主詞，較正式）

TOEFL 寫作中用 `It is + adj + to V` 更常見：

- **It is** essential **to** understand the context.
- **It is** difficult **to** predict long-term outcomes.
