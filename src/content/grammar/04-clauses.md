## 什麼是子句？

子句（clause）= **有主詞 + 有動詞**的結構。跟片語（phrase）的差別是：片語沒有主詞或動詞。

- 片語：*the scientist with red hair*（沒有動詞）
- 子句：*the scientist **who has** red hair*（有主詞 who + 動詞 has）

一個句子可以有多個子句，用連接詞或關係代名詞串起來。

---

## 關係子句（Relative Clauses）

用來修飾名詞，說明「哪一個」或「補充說明」。

### who / that — 修飾人

- Scientists **who study** marine life often dive deep.
- The researcher **that conducted** the study is from MIT.

### which / that — 修飾事物

- The theory, **which was proposed in 1905**, changed physics.
- The data **that we collected** support this hypothesis.

### 限定 vs 非限定

`which` 加逗號 → **補充說明**（拿掉也沒關係）

`that` 沒逗號 → **限定說明**（說明是哪一個，不能拿掉）

- The experiment **that failed** → 特指那個失敗的實驗（限定）
- The experiment, **which failed**, ... → 補充提到它失敗了（非限定）

### whose — 表所屬

- Scientists **whose research** focuses on genetics...
- A species **whose habitat** has been destroyed...

---

## 名詞子句（Noun Clauses）

整個子句當名詞用，可以當主詞或受詞。

### that 子句

- Researchers believe **that climate is changing**.
- It is clear **that more funding is needed**.

### whether / if 子句（表達不確定）

- Scientists debated **whether the data were reliable**.
- It remains unclear **whether the treatment works**.

### wh- 子句

- The study explains **why coral reefs are bleaching**.
- We do not yet know **how the virus spreads**.

---

## 副詞子句（Adverb Clauses）

修飾動詞，說明時間、原因、讓步、條件。

### 時間

- **When** the temperature drops, ice forms.
- **After** the experiment was completed, results were analyzed.
- **Once** the data were collected, the team began analysis.

### 原因

- **Because** the sample was too small, the results were inconclusive.
- **Since** resources were limited, they focused on key variables.

### 讓步（儘管…）

- **Although** the theory was widely accepted, new evidence challenged it.
- **Even though** costs were high, the project continued.

### 條件

- **If** the temperature rises, the ice will melt.
- **Unless** action is taken, the situation will worsen.

---

## 平行結構（Parallel Structure）

連接詞（and / or / but）連接的成分必須**詞性相同**。

- ✓ The study is **accurate**, **thorough**, and **well-documented**.
- ✗ The study is **accurate**, **thorough**, and **well documented it**.

- ✓ She enjoys **reading**, **writing**, and **conducting** research.
- ✗ She enjoys **reading**, **writing**, and **to conduct** research.

`not only...but also` 也要對稱：

- ✓ Not only **did they collect** data, but they also **analyzed** it.
- ✗ Not only **collecting** data, but also they **analyzed** it.

---

## 分詞片語（Participle Phrases）

分詞片語（-ing 或 p.p. 開頭）放在句首時，它修飾的對象**必須是主要子句的主詞**。

如果邏輯上做這個動作的不是主詞，就叫做「懸垂修飾語」（dangling modifier），是常見文法錯誤。

- ✓ **Having reviewed the data**, the team published the results.
  （team 審查了資料 ✓）

- ✗ **Having reviewed the data**, the results were published.
  （results 沒辦法「審查」資料 ✗）

- ✓ **Collected from three sites**, the samples were analyzed in the lab.
  （samples 被收集 ✓ — 被動分詞）

- ✗ **Collected from three sites**, the researchers analyzed the samples.
  （researchers 不是被收集的 ✗）

- ✓ **Working in remote areas**, researchers often face communication challenges.
  （researchers 在遠地工作 ✓）

> 遇到句首分詞片語，立刻找主要子句的主詞，問：「是這個主詞在做（或被做）這個動作嗎？」
