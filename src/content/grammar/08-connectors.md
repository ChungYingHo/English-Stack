## 為什麼連接詞很重要

TOEFL 閱讀中，理解「句子之間的邏輯關係」是答題關鍵。連接詞就是作者給你的邏輯信號，告訴你接下來是「補充」還是「反駁」還是「因果」。

連接詞分三大類，用法不同：

- **連接詞（Conjunction）**：連接兩個子句，中間不加分號。如：*although, because, while*
- **副詞連接詞（Conjunctive Adverb）**：放在新句子開頭（或中間），前面通常加分號或句號。如：*however, therefore, moreover*
- **介系詞片語**：後面接名詞或動名詞，不接子句。如：*despite, because of, due to*

---

## 對比 & 讓步（儘管 / 但是）

### 連接詞（接子句）

- **although / even though / though** — 儘管…（強調後面的主句）
  - **Although** the sample was small, the results were significant.

- **while / whereas** — 兩者對比（A 是這樣，B 是那樣）
  - Mammals are warm-blooded, **while** reptiles are cold-blooded.

### 副詞連接詞（放新句子開頭）

- **however** — 然而，轉折
  - The theory was widely accepted. **However**, new evidence challenges it.

- **nevertheless / nonetheless** — 儘管如此（讓步後仍堅持）
  - The conditions were harsh. **Nevertheless**, the team completed the study.

- **on the other hand** — 另一方面
  - Solar energy is clean. **On the other hand**, it requires significant infrastructure.

- **in contrast / by contrast** — 相比之下
  - Developed nations emit more CO₂. **In contrast**, developing nations face more severe impacts.

### 介系詞片語（後接名詞/動名詞）

- **despite / in spite of** — 儘管（後接名詞）
  - **Despite** the limited funding, the research was completed.
  - ✗ Despite **they had** limited funding...（despite 後不能接子句）
  - ✓ Despite **having** limited funding...（接動名詞可以）

## 因果關係

### 表「原因」（因為）

- **because** / **since** / **as**（連接詞，接子句）
  - **Because** the sample size was too small, the results were inconclusive.
  - **Since** no control group was used, the findings are questionable.

- **because of** / **due to** / **owing to**（介系詞片語，接名詞）
  - The experiment was delayed **due to** equipment failure.
  - ✗ Due to **the equipment failed**...（後面不接子句）

### 表「結果」（因此）

- **therefore** / **thus** / **hence** / **as a result** / **consequently**
  - The data were incomplete. **Therefore**, no conclusions could be drawn.
  - Resources were limited; **thus**, the team had to prioritize.

- **so**（連接詞，口語）
  - The sample was contaminated, **so** the experiment was repeated.

### result in vs result from（重溫）

- Industrial emissions **result in** air pollution.（A 導致 B → result in）
- Air pollution **results from** industrial emissions.（B 起因於 A → result from）

## 遞進 & 補充（而且、此外）

- **furthermore** / **moreover** / **in addition** / **additionally**
  - The method is cost-effective. **Furthermore**, it is environmentally friendly.
  - The study was thorough. **Moreover**, it was replicated in three countries.

- **also** / **besides** / **as well as**
  - The treatment reduced pain. It **also** improved mobility.
  - **Besides** reducing cost, the new method improves accuracy.

---

## 舉例 & 說明

- **for example** / **for instance**
  - Many animals migrate seasonally. **For example**, Arctic terns travel from pole to pole.

- **such as** / **including**（後接名詞，不用完整子句）
  - Marine mammals, **such as** dolphins and whales, rely on echolocation.

- **specifically** / **in particular**
  - Climate change affects biodiversity. **In particular**, coral reefs are under threat.

- **that is** / **i.e.** / **namely**（重新定義或說明）
  - The study used a double-blind method — **that is**, neither the participants nor the researchers knew who received the treatment.

---

## 讓步後強調（雖然…但…）

- **admittedly** — 不可否認地（承認對方的點）
  - **Admittedly**, the sample size was small.

- **it is true that...but** — 確實…但…
  - **It is true that** the experiment has limitations, **but** the findings are still significant.

---

## 總結 & 結論

- **in conclusion** / **in summary** / **to summarize**
  - **In conclusion**, the evidence supports the hypothesis.

- **overall** / **in general**
  - **Overall**, the results suggest a positive correlation.

## 快速辨認邏輯關係

閱讀時看到這些詞，立刻判斷作者的意圖：

| 看到這個 | 意思是 |
|----------|--------|
| however / but / yet | 前後矛盾，準備反駁 |
| therefore / thus / hence | 前面是原因，這裡是結論 |
| although / even though | 讓步，但主句才是重點 |
| furthermore / moreover | 繼續加碼，方向一致 |
| for example / such as | 舉例，是補充說明不是新論點 |
| in contrast / whereas | 兩個東西做比較 |
