## P2. Take an Interview 面試式問答

**情境說明：** You have agreed to participate in a research study about language learning. You will have a short online interview with a researcher. The researcher will ask you some questions.

---

### Q1 — Personal Experience

Thank you for joining the study. Today, I'd like to ask you some questions about language learning. First, have you or someone you know ever tried to learn a foreign language? What method did you or they use, such as classes, apps, or conversation practice?

---

### Q2 — Preference / Hypothetical Choice

I see. Imagine you want to learn a new language. You can take a structured course with a teacher, which provides guidance but follows a fixed schedule, or use a self-paced app, which is flexible but requires strong motivation. Which would you choose, and why?

---

### Q3 — Agree or Disagree

Interesting. Next, I'd like to get your opinion. Some people believe that the best way to learn a language is to live in a country where it is spoken, rather than studying it in a classroom. Do you agree or disagree? Why or why not?

---

### Q4 — Positive and Negative Effects

Good points. Lastly, AI translation tools are becoming increasingly accurate, allowing people to communicate across languages instantly. How could the widespread use of real-time translation technology affect language learning in positive and negative ways? Explain your answer.
