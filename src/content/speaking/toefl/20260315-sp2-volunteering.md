## P2. Take an Interview 面試式問答

**情境說明：** You have volunteered for a research study about community involvement. You will have a short online interview with a researcher. The researcher will ask you some questions.

---

### Q1 — Personal Experience

Thank you for speaking with me today. I'd like to ask you some questions about volunteering and community service. First, have you or anyone you know ever volunteered for a community project or organization? What kind of work was it?

---

### Q2 — Preference / Hypothetical Choice

I see. Imagine you have a free weekend and want to volunteer. You can help at a local food bank, where the work is physical but the impact is immediate, or mentor a student online, where the work is less visible but may have a long-term effect. Which would you choose, and why?

---

### Q3 — Agree or Disagree

Interesting. Next, I'd like to get your opinion. Some people believe that universities and high schools should require students to complete a certain number of volunteer hours in order to graduate. Do you agree or disagree? Why or why not?

---

### Q4 — Positive and Negative Effects

Good points. Lastly, many organizations now coordinate volunteer work through online platforms and apps. How could the shift to digitally organized volunteering affect communities in positive and negative ways? Explain your answer.
