## P2. Take an Interview 面試式問答

**情境說明：** You have agreed to participate in a research study about pets and animal care. You will have a short online interview with a researcher. The researcher will ask you some questions.

---

### Q1 — Personal Experience

Thank you for participating today. I'd like to ask you some questions about pets and animal care. First, do you or does anyone in your family currently have a pet? If so, what kind of pet, and if not, would you like to have one? Why?

---

### Q2 — Preference / Hypothetical Choice

I see. Imagine you decide to get a pet. You can adopt a rescue animal from a shelter, which may have unknown health history but needs a home, or buy a specific breed from a breeder, which is predictable but more expensive. Which would you choose, and why?

---

### Q3 — Agree or Disagree

Interesting. Next, I'd like to get your opinion. Some people believe that keeping pets in small apartments is unfair to the animals. Do you agree or disagree? Why or why not?

---

### Q4 — Positive and Negative Effects

Good points. Lastly, some cities are creating more pet-friendly public spaces, such as off-leash parks and pet-friendly cafes and offices. How could making cities more pet-friendly affect residents in positive and negative ways? Explain your answer.
