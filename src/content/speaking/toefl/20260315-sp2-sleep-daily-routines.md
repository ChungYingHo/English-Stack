## P2. Take an Interview 面試式問答

**情境說明：** You have volunteered for a research study about sleep habits and daily routines. You will have a short online interview with a researcher. The researcher will ask you some questions.

---

### Q1 — Personal Experience

Thank you for taking part in this study. Today, I'd like to ask you some questions about your sleep habits and daily routines. First, do you consider yourself a morning person who wakes up early, or a night person who stays up late? Why?

---

### Q2 — Preference / Hypothetical Choice

I see. Imagine you have a busy week ahead. You can sleep fewer hours each night to have more time for work and activities, or keep a full eight hours of sleep but have less free time. Which would you choose, and why?

---

### Q3 — Agree or Disagree

Interesting. Next, I'd like to get your opinion. Some people believe that schools and workplaces should start later in the morning to allow people to get more sleep. Do you agree or disagree? Why or why not?

---

### Q4 — Positive and Negative Effects

Good points. Lastly, wearable devices that track sleep patterns and suggest bedtime routines are becoming more common. How could widespread use of sleep-tracking technology affect people's daily lives in positive and negative ways? Explain your answer.
